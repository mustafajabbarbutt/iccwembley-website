Collection Appeals

How to Apply

Assalamu 'Alaikum. If your masjid or charitable organisation wishes to conduct a collection at ICC Wembley, please send your request by email to appeals@iccwembley.co.uk. A member of the office will review your request and get back to you shortly, insha'Allah.

Please ensure the following information is included in your email:

  • 1Request Letter — a formal letter from your organisation requesting permission to collect
  • 2Charity Registration Certificate — proof that your organisation is a registered charity
  • 3Project collecting for — details of the specific project or cause funds will go towards
  • 4Country or area you propose to collect for — specify the geographic beneficiary
  • 5Contact Name — the name of the person we should correspond with
  • 6Contact Number — a phone number for the designated contact
Once we receive your email, a member of the office will get back to you shortly, insha'Allah.
Send Your Application

Please send your request along with all required documents to our appeals team. Make sure to include all the information listed above in your email.
