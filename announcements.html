Wembley Event Day Parking - Fridays Only

Important
Posted: 29 April 2026

Assalamu Alaikum dear brothers and sisters,

Please be aware that on Fridays during Wembley event days, there will be ease of parking restrictions around the masjid.

Time Window:

  • 12:30 PM - 2:30 PM (Fridays only, on event days)

Important Reminder:

  • Please park responsibly
  • Do NOT block driveways
  • Be considerate of our neighbours

This applies specifically to Fridays when there are events at Wembley Stadium. Please ensure you are mindful of parking regulations and local residents.

JazakAllah Khair for your cooperation.

Fridays only: 12:30 PM - 2:30 PM on event days

Qurbani 2026 - Perform Your Sacrifice in India

Urgent
Posted: 27 April 2026

Assalamu Alaikum dear brothers and sisters,

We are pleased to announce our Qurbani program for 2026. Perform your Qurbani in India and support those in need.

Pricing:

  • Full Cow (7 shares): £190
  • Single Share: £45

Rewards: The person performing Qurbani will be rewarded for every hair on the body of the animal or for every fibre of wool in the case of sheep.

For more information or to book your Qurbani, please contact the masjid office at 0208 903 3760.

May Allah accept your sacrifice and grant you immense reward. Taqabbal Allahu minna wa minkum.
