Our Story

A Home for the Muslim Community in Wembley

The Islamic Cultural Centre Wembley (ICC Wembley) has been a cornerstone of the local Muslim community for decades. We are a welcoming place of worship, learning, and community service, rooted in the teachings of Islam and dedicated to serving Muslims and non-Muslims alike.

Situated in the heart of Wembley, our centre provides a full range of Islamic services — from daily prayers and Friday Jumu'ah to our Madrassa for children, funeral services, and community outreach. We strive to create an environment where faith, knowledge, and brotherhood flourish.

What We Stand For

Our Pillars

🕌
Prayer & Worship

We offer all five daily prayers, Friday Jumu'ah, and Tarawih during Ramadan in a peaceful and welcoming environment.

📖
Education

Our Madrassa provides Islamic education for children, teaching Qur'an recitation, Arabic, and Islamic studies.

🤝
Community Service

We support our community through charitable activities, collection appeals, and connecting people in need with resources.

🌿
Funeral Services

We provide complete Islamic funeral services including ghusl, janazah prayers, burial arrangements, and repatriation support.
